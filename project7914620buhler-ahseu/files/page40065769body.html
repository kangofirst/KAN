The main advantages of Buhler-AHS system
Air humidification is crucial for maintaining health, prolonging the lifespan of appliances, musical instruments, and manufacturing equipment. It also plays a vital role in certain technological processes in industries like polygraphy and food production. Buhler-AHS is a leading manufacturer of advanced adiabatic air humidification systems that find applications in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.

Short description of Buhler-AHS systems
Buhler-AHS systems are designed to adapt to specific operating conditions and customer requirements without the need for complex installation processes.

These systems incorporate the latest advancements in hydraulic systems, water purification, and disinfection. By utilizing innovative technological schemes and avoiding heating elements and intricate electrical components, Buhler-AHS ensures maximum cost-effectiveness, reliability, and reduced maintenance expenses.
Advantages over systems of other types
  • Allows precise humidity control in each room

  • Prevents the formation of microflora due to sterile water supply

  • Eliminates dust from dissolved salts through water demineralization, and produces small water droplets that quickly absorb into the air without causing moisture on surfaces

  • Additionally, the high-pressure operation eliminates the need for additional fans, resulting in a silent system.
  • The small diameter tubes make it possible to install the nozzles in already finished rooms

  • The system operates fully automatically

  • The system operates independently of ventilation

  • Ensuring low energy and operating costs.
